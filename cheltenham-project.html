Cheltenham Hotel

Complete Renovation & Decorating Project

Project Overview

Full renovation and decorating project for a hotel in Cheltenham. This comprehensive project included interior painting, decorative finishes, and exterior work to transform the property and create a welcoming environment for guests.

Exterior Transformation

Completed exterior painting giving the hotel a fresh, professional appearance that enhances curb appeal and welcomes guests.

Lobby & Common Areas

Interior painting and decorating in the hotel's main lobby and common areas, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for guests.

Guest Room Refresh

Complete redecorating of guest rooms with premium paints and finishes, ensuring a comfortable and stylish environment for hotel guests.

Corridors & Hallways

Painting and finishing work throughout the hotel's corridors, maintaining consistency in design and quality across all areas.

Exterior Detail Work

Premium exterior painting with weather-resistant finishes designed to withstand the elements and maintain the hotel's appearance for years to come.

Final Details

Attention to detail in the finishing touches, ensuring every aspect of the renovation meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
