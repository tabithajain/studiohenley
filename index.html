About Studio Henley

Studio Henley is a premium painting and decorating service based in Henley-on-Thames. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service.

We use only the highest quality materials and environmentally friendly products to ensure beautiful, long-lasting results for your home or business.

While we primarily serve Henley-on-Thames and the surrounding Oxfordshire area, we also venture to London and other locations for larger projects. Our team of skilled professionals is fully trained to handle projects of any size, from single rooms to entire properties.
