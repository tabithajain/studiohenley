London Mould Remediation

Mould Remediation & Decorating Project in Bayswater, London

Project Overview

Complete mould removal and treatment for a residential flat in London's Bayswater area. We identified the source of the mould, safely removed all affected areas, and applied preventive treatments. The project also included full redecorating to restore the flat to its original condition.

Mould Remediation Process

Identifying and safely removing mould from affected areas. Our team uses specialized equipment and treatments to ensure complete removal and prevent future growth.

Wall Preparation

After mould removal, walls are prepared for redecorating. This includes thorough cleaning, priming, and ensuring surfaces are ready for painting.

Final Finish

The completed room after redecorating. Fresh paint and clean surfaces create a healthy, beautiful living space free from mould.
